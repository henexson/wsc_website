About Me

Hi, I’m Scott Henexson. Woodworking has been a lifelong passion of mine, rooted in a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and creativity. With over 15 years of experience in construction and remodeling, I’ve spent the last 5 years focusing on fine woodworking—honing my skills and pushing my craft to new levels.

From cutting boards and kitchen tables to custom barn doors and dog kennels, I take pride in building high-quality, handcrafted pieces that are both functional and beautiful. Every project is approached with hard work, attention to detail, and a commitment to exceptional customer service.

If you can imagine it, I’d love to bring it to life. Let’s create something amazing together!
